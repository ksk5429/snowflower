# Disclosure templates

snowflower is **independent editorial coverage**. Every published piece must carry a disclosure footer in both languages where the audience may be bilingual.

These templates are derived from:

- **US:** FTC 16 CFR § 255 (Endorsement Guides), 16 CFR § 465 (Native Advertising)
- **Korea:** 표시·광고의 공정화에 관한 법률 (표시광고법), 추천·보증 등에 관한 광고 심사지침 (KFTC)
- **EU:** Digital Services Act (DSA) Art. 26 (transparent advertising)
- **Platform overlays:** YouTube "altered or synthetic" toggle, Instagram "Paid partnership" tag, TikTok "Promotional content" disclosure, Meta "AI Info" label

---

## When disclosure is required

| Situation | US FTC | KR KFTC | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| No relationship with covered brand, no comp | Not strictly required, but recommended | Not required | Use **Neutral** footer |
| Free sample / loaner unit received | **Required** (material connection) | 필수 | Use **Sample-disclosed** footer |
| Paid sponsorship / affiliate link | **Required** | 필수 | Use **Sponsored** footer + platform "paid partnership" tag |
| AI-generated voice / image / video that looks realistic | **Required** under YT/IG/TikTok rules | 권장 | Toggle platform AI label + add line in description |
| Direct quote of brand's medical/efficacy claim | Attribute to brand | 출처 표기 | Use **Attribution** template |

## Templates

### Neutral (default — no relationship, no comp)

**EN:**
> snowflower is independent editorial. We are not affiliated with the brands covered in this piece, and we received no compensation, samples, or pre-publication review from any of them. Coverage decisions are ours alone. See snowflower.tech/about for our editorial policy.

**KR:**
> snowflower(눈꽃)은 독립 에디토리얼 채널입니다. 본 콘텐츠에 등장하는 어떤 브랜드와도 협찬·후원·샘플 제공·사전 검토 관계가 없으며, 모든 편집 결정은 자체적으로 이루어졌습니다. 자세한 편집 방침은 snowflower.tech/about을 참고하세요.

### Sample-disclosed (free unit received, no other comp)

**KR:**
> 알림: 본 리뷰에 사용된 [Product]는 [Brand]로부터 검토용으로 무상 제공받았습니다. 별도의 금전적 대가는 없으며, 사전 검수 또한 진행되지 않았습니다. 편집권은 snowflower에 있습니다.

## Hard rules — never violate

1. **No medical claims about MouthPad (or any covered device) without direct attribution to the brand's published source.** No "improves quality of life by X%" without a citation. No "FDA-cleared" unless verified against the FDA database. No clinical-outcome claims unless quoted from peer-reviewed work.
2. **No use of "augmental" or "MouthPad" as account name, handle, or logo.** Nominative fair use covers referring to the product in editorial; it does not cover branding.
3. **Never imply affiliation, partnership, or sponsorship that does not exist.**
4. **Disclosure goes in the post body / video description, not just the bio.** Bio-only disclosure does not meet FTC standards as of 2023 staff guidance; KFTC similarly requires per-content disclosure.
5. **Korean platforms:** Naver and Kakao both penalize undisclosed promotional content separately from KFTC enforcement. Disclosure is both legally required AND algorithmically protective.
