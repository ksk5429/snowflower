## Positioning rules (non-negotiable)

- **"Nothing about us without us" operationalized:** every device review features the actual disabled user driving it on camera with editorial control. Paid disabled co-host is structural.
- **Cover competitors fairly.** Tobii, Quha, Glassouse, AssistiveTouch all in editorial scope.
- **Language: ask the individual.** Defaults vary (Deaf/Autistic/Blind = identity-first; SMA/CP/MS = often person-first). Per-subject.
- **Trigger pattern auto-reject:** inspiration porn, cure framing, "overcoming," before/after as transformation, ableist metaphors, unsolicited tragedy backstory, "high/low-functioning," "wheelchair-bound."
- **Reference frame:** Apple's *The Greatest* (Dec 2022) + *I'm Not Remarkable* (May 2025) — show ordinary use, no pity score, no slow piano.

## Content production model

**Cadence (sustainable solo):**
- 1 deep YouTube hero piece per week
- 3–5 platform-shaped Shorts/Reels/TikToks per hero piece (repurposed)
- 2 LinkedIn posts per week (contrarian or stat hook, ≤200 chars pre-truncate)
- Daily Bluesky presence
- 1 Beehiiv newsletter per week
- 1 Naver Blog (KR, manual) per month

**Pre-batch 4 pieces before launch.** Weeks 1–4 buffered.

**Editing = 40% of total time.** Outsource trigger: when revenue covers $50–150/video freelancer.

**AI-assist line:**
- Safe: ideation, outline, transcript clean, B-roll search, thumbnail iteration, title A/B, captions (Whisper)
- Slop line: AI writes final prose without human voice rewrite, synthetic claims, deepfake voice, generic AI thumbnails

## Monetization model

**Revenue pyramid (planned):**

```
            augmental (or Tobii/Quha) anchor sponsor
            $3K–$10K/month at month 6+
                     ▲
        Patreon/membership for clinics/schools
        $15–25/mo · ~50 subs = $1K/mo
                     ▲
   Beehiiv ad-network + selective brand placements
   $40–80/mo at 1,200 subs · $200–400/placement at 2,500+
                     ▲
       YouTube AdSense + Amazon AT affiliate
       $50–200/mo at 1k–10k subs (FLOOR, not strategy)
```

**Key:** AdSense is the floor, not the strategy. SBSK exited ad-dependency by becoming a 501(c)(3) funded by Patreon ($25K+/mo). Plan revenue as Patreon + earned-media partnerships + book/course/speaking — AdSense is bonus.

**Sponsor rate floors when you start charging:**
- Newsletter (B2B clinician density): $200–400/placement at 1k–2.5k subs
- LinkedIn post: $500–1,500 at 5k followers
- YouTube anchor sponsor: $500–2,000/video at 5k–25k subs
- **Price by audience density (% verified OT/SLP/AT-buyer), not raw CPM.**

## B2B leverage moves (what moves augmental)

### 1. The 30-day-trial editorial niche is unowned

OTs Google "MouthPad vs Quha vs Glassouse vs Tobii PCEye" when narrowing demo candidates. This is the editorial slot directly upstream of unit conversion.

### 2. Funding-path content is underserved

"How to get MouthPad covered by Medicaid waiver in [state]." Top-of-funnel for actual buyers. Tobii Dynavox built a whole funding-navigation team because this is the friction. Almost no creator does this well.

## Anti-patterns (auto-reject)

- CPM sponsorship as primary revenue
- Build-in-public before $1K MRR (= noise, not credibility)
- TikTok as launch platform (audit 5–10 days, "copy-from-other-platforms" ban)
- LinkedIn personal posts under brand-only fake name (ToS Art. 8.2 termination)
- Pitching augmental before 12 weeks of measurable audience overlap
